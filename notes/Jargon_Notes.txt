
-Floorsheet
    -both sales / purchase record
-Sales CM
    -sales payout information
        -information about the sales payment broker owes to its client
-Order
    -is solely for record keeping purposes, and doesn't intermingle with other models.

-Deal Cancel
    - strictly in purchase case
    - complement of Closeout

-Closeout
    - strictly in sales
    - complement of Deal Cancel
    - seen in CM report

-As they are complement of each other, the way they occur:
    -Sell order placed for shares not possessed.
      -If deal cancel when BrokerA -> Broker B,
        Broker A has to pay 120% the amount of order
        -The payment is known as closeout.
